
# Software Developer Portfolio

A bold, modern single-page portfolio website to showcase your skills and link from LinkedIn.

## 1. Hero Section
- Full-screen hero with your name, title (e.g. "Full-Stack Developer"), and a short tagline
- Bold typography with accent colors and subtle animations (fade-in, slide-up)
- Call-to-action buttons: "View My Work" and "Download CV"
- Links to LinkedIn, GitHub, and email

## 2. About Me
- Brief bio with a photo/avatar placeholder
- Key stats or highlights (years of experience, projects completed, etc.)
- Tech stack showcase with skill icons/badges

## 3. Experience Timeline
- Vertical timeline of work experience
- Each entry: company name, role, dates, and brief description
- Clean cards with hover effects

## 4. Projects Showcase
- Grid of project cards with images, titles, descriptions, and tech tags
- Links to live demos and GitHub repos
- Hover animations for a modern feel

## 5. Contact Section
- Contact form (name, email, message) with validation
- Social links (LinkedIn, GitHub, email)
- Simple footer with copyright

## 6. Design & Polish
- Bold color palette with vibrant accent colors
- Smooth scroll navigation with a sticky header
- Fully responsive (mobile-friendly for LinkedIn mobile viewers)
- Subtle scroll-triggered animations throughout
